SHIPPING & RETURNS 

            Shipping  Policy 

            When placing an order, you represent that you are 21 years of age or older and that the shipping 
            address is also 21 years of age or older. An adult signature is required upon delivery.
            Most orders she processes within 24 hours. However, some orders may take an additional 1-3 
            business days from order to processing. On rare occasions, there may be unexpected delays in the 
            delivery of your order. In this case, we will notify you by email.
            It is the customer's responsibility to be present at the time of delivery for an adult's signature. If no 
            one is available to pick up the package, another delivery will be attempted.


            Return & Exchange Policy

            We work with local retailers to fulfill orders and stores are responsible for processing returns.We 
            encourage you to contact the store for their return process and policy. Please note that each 
            store may have its own return policy. Contact information can be found in your order confirmation email 
            and on the shop page.

            We will refund your money if you receive the wrong or damaged item. In that case, the store will 
            collect the item and return it. Returns are generally accepted in store within 5 days of purchase. 
            Refunds typically take 5-10 business days after the product is returned to be processed and 
            credited to your credit card account. The number of days also varies by bank.
